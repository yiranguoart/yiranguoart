Höuse

Höuse is a special-bonded Risograph book. It began with a repeated house image, returning in different forms, sometimes folded, sometimes upside down. It is accompanied by unsettling, otherworldly scenes, and characters—sometimes cutouts, sometimes behind the windows—that can be considered the tenants of this haunted house, or visitors to viewers’ dreams.

Size: 10x11inches. Year: 2022. Available for purchase at Printed Matter.

Hallowzine

Hallowzine is a tiny version of Höuse, a single sheet booklet Risograph-printed with four colours. The reverse side is a duotone Risograph print revealed when fully unfolded.

Size: 4.25x5.5inches. Year: 2019. Available for purchase at Printed Matter.

Cortado Man

Cortado Man is a Riso printed accordion zine. The book unfolds into one hand-sized accordion page, bound by another smaller accordion page, filled with many big and small cortado men. All men poured in Shanghai.

Size: 5x7inches. Year: 2024. Available for purchase at Printed Matter.
