A music video for musician Temple Rat (Mei Yuxin), for the title track on her EP The Composition of Air (Siamese Twins Records, 2023). Inspired by the organic synthesizers and field recordings used in the track, the video uses raw footage from a train ride across China, together with hand drawn animation, to depict the elements of speed and time lingering in the music.
