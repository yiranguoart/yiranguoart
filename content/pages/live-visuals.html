Samples of live visuals to accompany musical performances in Shanghai. The visuals are composed of found footage, assembled on the fly into moving collages. Many of these performances were hosted at Penumbra (魑魅魍魉), a monthly night for experimental music and film that I co-organise. Musical guests at Penumbra have included Kode9, Chris SSG, Knopha, Temple Rat, and otay:onii.
